What Do We Do?

We help to get food to the people in need


Every single year, around 9 million people die of hunger in the U.S. Yet, millions of restaurants and entertainment services decide to throw out and waste the food they have left at the end of the day.


At Food Where It Should Be, we have created a system that allows restaurants to insert what food and how much they have left over every single day. From this, shelters and food banks can esaily pinpoint restaurants in their area using Google Maps to pick up the extra food from the restaurants.
