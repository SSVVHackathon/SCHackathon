Why?


Every single year, around 9 million people die of hunger in the U.S. Yet, restuarnats and other entertainment services throw out hundreds of pounds of food at the end of each day. Why isn't this food donated to local shelters and food banks? Thats where we come in.



So What Do We Do?


We connect restaurants to local shelters and food banks. Restaurants can easily input their location and how much food they have left at the end of the day. Then, local shelters and food banks can pick up the food to ensure no wastage.
